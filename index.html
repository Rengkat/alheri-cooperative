About Us

Our Story, Vision
& Purpose

Rooted in the rich agricultural landscape of Taraba State, Alheri Cattle Multi-Purpose Co-operative Society Limited was established to harness the collective power of livestock farmers and create lasting economic change.

Who We Are

A Co-operative Rooted in Community

Alheri Cattle Multi-Purpose Co-operative Society Limited (ACMPCSL) is a registered co-operative society headquartered at No. Gombe Street, Turaki A' Ward, Jalingo, Taraba State. We were established with the singular mission of transforming the livelihoods of livestock farmers and agricultural entrepreneurs through co-operative principles.

Our society brings together cattle farmers, smallholders, traders, and agribusiness stakeholders under a unified platform where resources are pooled, risks are shared, and prosperity is built collectively. We operate across multiple Local Government Areas in Taraba State, with a growing membership that continues to benefit from our expanding range of services.

Grounded in the seven principles of co-operation — voluntary membership, democratic member control, member economic participation, autonomy, education, co-operation among co-operatives, and concern for community — we deliver real, tangible value to every member.

As a multi-purpose society, we do not limit ourselves to one service. We offer cattle fattening, financial services, input supply, veterinary support, market linkage, training, and more — making us a one-stop hub for agricultural development in our region.

What We Offer

Our Core Service Areas

🐄
Cattle Fattening Program
Core Service

Our flagship cattle fattening program uses scientifically developed feeding protocols and expert management to bring your cattle to premium market weight in optimal time.

  • Expert nutritional feeding schedules
  • Health monitoring & disease prevention
  • Growth tracking and records management
  • Group fattening for shared costs
  • Market-ready slaughter assessment
💰
Agricultural Finance & Loans
Financial Services

Access affordable credit facilities tailored specifically for livestock farmers. Our loan products are designed with flexible repayment schedules aligned with farming cycles.

  • Low-interest member loans (10–15% p.a.)
  • Seasonal credit for farm inputs
  • Emergency loan facilities
  • Member savings & deposit schemes
  • Annual dividend on share capital
🏥
Veterinary & Health Services
Animal Health

Subsidized veterinary care is available to all registered members, including scheduled herd visits, emergency response, and preventive medicine programs.

  • Scheduled farm veterinary visits
  • Vaccination & immunization programs
  • Deworming & parasite control
  • Artificial insemination services
  • Emergency veterinary response
🛒
Market Linkage & Trade
Market Access

We eliminate exploitative middlemen by connecting our members directly to verified buyers, abattoirs, processing plants, and regional livestock markets.

  • Direct buyer-seller matchmaking
  • Collective bargaining for better prices
  • Transport & logistics coordination
  • Market price intelligence sharing
  • Export linkage partnerships

Input Supply & Equipment
Farm Inputs

Through bulk purchasing power, we supply quality farm inputs — feeds, medications, equipment — at significantly reduced prices to registered members.

  • Quality feed concentrates & supplements
  • Agricultural chemicals & medicines
  • Farm tools & equipment hire
  • Subsidized prices for members
  • Delivery to farm gate available

Training & Capacity Building
